\section{Concept}
\begin{frame}{Concept}
    \begin{block}{Image}
        An image is something which represents brain properties of brain in picture format;
    \end{block}  \pause

    \begin{block}{Map}
        A spatial representation of the specific brain properties.
    \end{block}  \pause

    \begin{block}{Atlas}
        A structural framework in which individual brain maps can integrated.
    \end{block} \pause

    \begin{block}{Database}
        A system to archive and manage brain atlas and individual brain maps.
    \end{block}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}{Criteria}
    \begin{enumerate}
           \item 依据被试激活cluster和解剖结构，group level parcellation 的相对位置；
           \item 依据被试激活cluster间的相对位置：
           \item 确保每个被试的激活阈限统一；
           \item 连续的cluster 是必要条件，若在一个group label 中，有两个连续的cluster，则选择一个更可能的；
           \item 范围在group level parcellation 边缘或只有部分相交，依据和其它脑区的相对判定，该cluster是否入选；
           \item 可以通过调整被试激活图阈限，从最有把握的cluster中心开始选择，然后降低阈限递增添加，直至最终阈限；
    \end{enumerate}
\end{frame}

\section{Way station}
\begin{frame}{Way station}
    Dividing the brain is more art than science; there is long way to go, we should attack it in phase-wise manner.
    Where is our way station ?
        \begin{enumerate}

          \item Group parces guided region parcellation;

          \item Connectivity atlas: ...
